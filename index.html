About WyzLink

WyzLink is a Seattle based leading VR/AR education platform and solutions provider with a focus on professional medical and nursing education.

WyzLink was found by a group of believers who use AR/VR technology to change the world. They are building a Medical Simulation platform to use generated virtual medical environment, virtual medical equipment, virtual medical personnel and even the virtual patient to train the medical students or nurse students to learn by exploring without jeopardize the patient safety.
